CONTACT

Hi! My name is Olenka. I'm in my first year of the Specialization in Computation Arts at Concordia. Despite having a rather strong background in visual arts, I enjoyed coding this site! My passion for motion design and worldbuilding are what inspired the concept for this website. Also, I am very fond of the dessert aesthetic!


On this fantastical site, you can browse through the spirits' current menu of desserts, which are named by their puns. You may also play the card game to help the spirits collect and organize their dishes. (Yes, the spirits are surprisingly unorganized.) Just don't forget to receive your fortune on your way out!


Thank you for stopping by.
