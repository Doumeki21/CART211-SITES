    HISTORY OF CERN:

  • Tim-Berner's Lee - An Oxford graduate, worked at CERN. He developed a program named ENQUIRE.
  • March 1989 - Tim wrote the first proposal for the World Wide Web.
  • End of 1990 - Tim had the first Web server + browser running at CERN.
  • 1993 - CERN management decided the Web should be available for all to use. This decision was what allowed all of us to have internet access today.

    COLTAN MINING IN EASTERN CONGO (2016):

  • Coltan can be found in our everyday devices.
  • Everyone including pregnant women and children are forced to do heavy labor and dig up Coltan under dangerous working conditions (ex. Bare foot, unsanitary water, poor working tools.)
  • Unsanitary water and air often lead to unhealthy lives (ex. tumors from drinking water, very sick babies, etc.)
    • These workers earn as little as $2 a day.
    • The ugly reality is that We are driving this, because we all want cheap products.
    • Companies are also doing so because they think they can put their money to "better use".

    JOHN BERGER'S 1972 DOCUMENTARY SERIES: WAYS OF SEEING:

  • Paintings at the beginning of their time had much more meaning to where they were located. We had to go to them (ex. The Church). Nowadays, these paintings can be found through any of our screens.The paintings come to us.
  • "When these paintings are being duplicated or viewed through a camera or film, a close-up or detailed view of the painting can completely change the whole meaning of the painting itself. The parts of the painting are arranged into a narrative which unfolds depending on their showtime (rather the original painting unfolds everything simultaneously)."
  • The context of a painting can also change from its other surrounding images/ media/ music and the things presented before and after it.

  • Trade secrets:If a trade secret is acquired by another company, it could harm the original holder.

  • Copyrights:used to protect expressions of ideas and creations (ex. art and writing).
